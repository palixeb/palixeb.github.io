    虎穴に入らずんば虎子を得ず

    nothing ventured nothing gained

    • manny marx

    Posted on Nov 20, 2015 at 12:20 AM

    So this is it. After weeks of watching YouTube videos, combing through tomes of stackoverflow posts, reading a ton of blogs / articles, and very late nights / early mornings. It's DONE.

    ... well kind of...it's done for the most part.... mannymarx.com has been something I have wanted to do for awhile; that is to build a website as well as connect creatively with people around the world. It feels so great to finally have this site up, running, and responsive—I hope. What feels even greater is being able to make music again.

    mannymarx.com is also a place for creators to have their creations displayed with the purpose of creating a network of creative people. I will share them here on this blog. If you have anything your like me to listen to, play, watch, or read contact me here, and I'll review it on here.

    Well, if you are in need of a looping tune for menu music, a beep, a plop, a bop, a full out song, or anything else, contact me and let's make these sounds a reality!

    Peace

    mmx
