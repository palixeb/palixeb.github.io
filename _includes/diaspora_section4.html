The Singularity

During The Great Plague the entire planet was in a mad dash for a cure or vaccine which in turn lead to the unbanning of all unethical scientific research. For the first time in ages research and experimentation were liberated from politics and, to some extent, ethics. In the matter of a few years a vaccine was not only created but a number of breakthroughs in biology, neuroscience, computing, and many other fields. One such breakthrough is what actually paved the way to The Singularity. In short, it was to upload minds into a database, let The Great Plague run its course, and then re-upload the minds in to healthy clones of themselves. This theory later became a reality in 2242 and the marriage of man and machine unlocked a new chapter in humankind's journey through the cosmos. Surprisingly those/the person where responsible choose no remain anonymous under the pseudonym "Nyame".
