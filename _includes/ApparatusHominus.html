Apparatus Hominus

Currently inputing a mind into a machine for an extended amount of time is a highly controversial topic. Experiencing a physical form that has no need for the basic functions biological lifeforms require has an array of effects on a person. Based on this stream that's probably not too controversial in the future. Further, it's apparent that long term exposure can led to some odd psychological patterns.
